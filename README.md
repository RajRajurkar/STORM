🚀 LiveRisk AI
Dynamic & Explainable Insurance Risk Intelligence System

📌 Overview
LiveRisk AI is an intelligent risk assessment platform that transforms traditional insurance underwriting into a dynamic, explainable, and interactive system.

Instead of static, one-time risk scores, our system:

Continuously evaluates risk

Explains decisions transparently

Predicts future risk trends

Enables real-time interaction via chatbot and Telegram bot

🎯 Problem Statement
Traditional insurance systems suffer from:

❌ Static risk assessment (one-time evaluation)

❌ Limited data usage (form-based inputs only)

❌ Lack of transparency (black-box decisions)

❌ No future risk prediction

💡 Our Solution
LiveRisk AI introduces:

✅ Multi-factor risk engine

✅ Explainable AI (SHAP-based insights)

✅ Scenario simulation (what-if analysis)

✅ Future risk prediction

✅ Conversational interface (Telegram bot + AI chat)

🧠 Key Features
1️⃣ Risk Engine
Calculates risk using:

Location

Claims

Age

Income

Lifestyle

Outputs a normalized risk score (0–1)

2️⃣ Explainable AI
Shows contribution of each factor

Example:

+18% Claims
+15% Location
-10% Income
Ensures transparency and trust

3️⃣ Scenario Simulator
Users can modify inputs (BMI, smoking, etc.)

Instantly see impact on risk

Enables decision-making before action

4️⃣ Future Prediction
Predicts risk trajectory over time

Provides insights like:

“Risk will increase in 3 months”

Helps shift from reactive → proactive systems

5️⃣ Continuous Monitoring
Tracks risk changes over time

Detects events like:

Claims

Lifestyle changes

6️⃣ Telegram Bot Integration 🤖
Allows users to:

Calculate risk via chat

Ask questions

Get explanations
